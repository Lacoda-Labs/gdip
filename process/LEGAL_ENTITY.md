# GeoDistricts DAO LLC - Legal Entity Formation

This document outlines the legal structure and formation requirements for the GeoDistricts DAO LLC under Wyoming's 2023 DAO law.

## Legal Entity Overview

The GeoDistricts DAO operates as a Wyoming DAO LLC, providing legal protection, liability limitations, and tax optimization for decentralized governance.

### Entity Details
- **Entity Name**: GeoDistricts DAO LLC
- **Entity Type**: Limited Liability Company (DAO variant)
- **Jurisdiction**: State of Wyoming
- **Formation Date**: [To be determined - post-formation]
- **EIN**: [To be obtained post-formation]

## Formation Requirements

### Articles of Organization
Filed with Wyoming Secretary of State:
- Entity name and type
- Registered agent information
- DAO operating agreement reference
- Initial member/manager information
- Duration (perpetual unless specified)

### Operating Agreement
DAO-specific operating agreement including:
- Governance structure and voting mechanisms
- Token holder rights and responsibilities
- Multi-sig signer duties and rotation procedures
- Treasury management policies
- Dispute resolution procedures
- Dissolution terms

### Registered Agent
Wyoming-based registered agent service for:
- Legal document service
- Annual report filing
- Compliance monitoring

## Multi-sig Governance Structure

### Initial Multi-sig Setup
- **Signers**: 5-7 individuals
  - Founding team representatives
  - Community-elected representatives
  - Independent advisors
- **Threshold**: 3-of-5 or 4-of-7 for standard transactions
- **Emergency Threshold**: 5-of-7 for constitutional changes

### Signer Responsibilities
- **Fiduciary Duty**: Act in best interest of the DAO
- **Transparency**: Regular reporting of activities
- **Security**: Hardware wallet usage, air-gapped operations
- **Rotation**: Term limits and community recall procedures

### Multi-sig Operations
- **Treasury Management**: Large transfers require multi-sig approval
- **Contract Deployments**: Smart contract upgrades and new deployments
- **Emergency Actions**: Security incident responses
- **Legal Actions**: Contract signing, regulatory compliance
